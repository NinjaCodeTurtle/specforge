# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want SpecForge to be a respectful, practical, and useful resource for everyone
who participates in the project. We are committed to providing a community free
of harassment and to making collaboration constructive, professional, and
inclusive.

## Expected Behavior

- Give and receive feedback respectfully.
- Explain technical disagreements with evidence and reasoning.
- Acknowledge mistakes and correct them quickly.
- Help other contributors understand repository conventions.
- Use welcoming and professional language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained antagonism.
- Publishing someone else's private information without permission.
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including GitHub issues,
pull requests, discussions, and any communication channels used for project work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate these
standards. They may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings,
temporary bans, or permanent removal from community spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
maintainers through GitHub. Include a description, links or screenshots, and
relevant context.
