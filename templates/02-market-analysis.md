<!-- TAM/SAM/SOM Framework:

     TAM (Total Addressable Market): The total revenue opportunity if you captured
     100% of the market with no constraints. Use top-down (industry reports) AND
     bottom-up (unit economics) approaches and compare.

     SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market): The portion of TAM you can realistically
     reach with your current business model, channels, and geography.

     SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market): The portion of SAM you can realistically
     capture in your planning horizon, given competition and resources.

     CALCULATION APPROACHES:
     - Top-down: Start with industry total, narrow by geography, segment, and relevance.
     - Bottom-up: (# of target customers) x (annual value per customer) = market size.
     - Value-theory: (# of users with the problem) x (value of solving it) = market.
     
     Best practice: Calculate using at least two approaches and triangulate. -->
